Dat NET views on the future of the web

The future of the web is heading in a very positive direction. 10 years ago, finding an efficient webpage for basic information was extremely rare. These days, with the newer innovations in HTML, CSS, and PHP, web designers are able to create websites in attractive styles and easy to use navigation.

With the advent of mobile applications, the future of web becomes even more important. Every single person at any given time is constantly searching the web for new information, whether it be news, celebrity gossip, or sports. Information is expanding daily with more and more people having access to the web and being able to share their voice. Simplifying how websites can be viewed on the mobile platform is going to be very crucial in the future.
